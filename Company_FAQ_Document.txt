Company FAQ – Orders, Shipping, Returns & Customer Support
Orders

Q: How can I check the status of my order?
You can check your order status by entering your Order ID in our chatbot or by contacting customer support. Order statuses include Processing, Shipped, Delivered, Pending, or Cancelled.

Q: Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
Orders can be modified or cancelled only while they are in the Processing stage. Once the order has been shipped, changes or cancellations are not possible.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
We accept major credit and debit cards, digital wallets, and approved corporate payment methods. Available options are shown at checkout.

Q: Can I reorder a past purchase?
Yes, you can reorder items from your previous orders by referencing your Order ID or through your account order history.

Q: Why was part of my order cancelled?
Partial cancellations may occur due to stock unavailability, payment issues, or shipping restrictions. You will be notified if any item in your order is cancelled.

Shipping & Delivery

Q: What shipping options are available?
We offer standard and expedited shipping. Options and costs are displayed during checkout based on your location.

Q: How long does delivery take?

Standard shipping: 5–7 business days

Expedited shipping: 2–3 business days
Delivery times may vary based on location and product availability.

Q: How can I track my shipment?
Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number and carrier details. You can track your shipment via the carrier’s website or through our chatbot.

Q: My order is delayed — what should I do?
If your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery date, please check the tracking link first. If the issue persists, contact customer support for assistance.

Q: My package shows delivered but I didn’t receive it.
Please check with neighbors or your building reception. If you still cannot locate the package, contact customer support within 48 hours.

Q: Can I change my delivery address after ordering?
Address changes are possible only before the order is shipped. Once shipped, delivery details cannot be modified.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to selected international destinations. Shipping fees, delivery timelines, and customs duties may vary by country.

Returns, Exchanges & Refunds

Q: What is your return policy?
Most items are eligible for return within 30 days of delivery, provided they are unused and in original packaging.

Q: How do I initiate a return?
You can initiate a return via our chatbot or by contacting customer support with your Order ID.

Q: Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?
Yes, exchanges are allowed for eligible items, subject to availability.

Q: When will I receive my refund?
Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected.

Q: What if my item is damaged or defective?
If your item arrives damaged or defective, please contact customer support immediately with photos of the issue.

Q: Are there items that cannot be returned?
Yes. Clearance items, personalized products, and digital goods are non-returnable unless defective.

Payments & Billing

Q: Why did my payment fail?
Payment failures may occur due to insufficient funds, incorrect details, or bank restrictions. Please try again or use a different payment method.

Q: Was my card charged multiple times?
In rare cases, temporary authorization holds may appear. If you see multiple charges, contact customer support for verification.

Q: Are taxes included in the price?
Taxes are calculated at checkout based on your location and are shown separately before payment confirmation.

Q: Can I get an invoice or receipt?
Yes, invoices and receipts are automatically sent to your registered email after purchase.

Products & Availability

Q: Is a product in stock?
Product availability is shown on the product page. Stock levels are updated in real time.

Q: When will out-of-stock items be available?
Restock timelines vary. You can enable notifications on the product page or contact support for updates.

Q: Do you offer bulk or corporate discounts?
Yes, bulk and corporate pricing may be available. Please contact our sales or support team for details.

Q: Are there alternative products available?
Yes, our chatbot or support team can suggest similar or alternative products.

Account & Support

Q: Do I need an account to place an order?
No, you can place an order as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders and manage returns easily.

Q: How do I reset my password?
Click on “Forgot Password” on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
You can reach us via chatbot, email, or phone. Support hours are Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM (local time).

Q: Can I speak to a human agent?
Yes, you can request to speak with a human agent through the chatbot or by contacting support directly.

Q: How do I raise a complaint?
You can raise a complaint by contacting customer support with your Order ID and issue details.

Policies & Trust

Q: What is your warranty policy?
Warranty coverage varies by product. Warranty details are provided on the product page or invoice.

Q: How is my data protected?
We use industry-standard security measures to protect customer data and comply with applicable privacy regulations.

Q: Are payments secure?
Yes, all payments are processed through secure, encrypted payment gateways.

Q: What are your terms and conditions?
Our terms and conditions are available on our website and apply to all purchases and services.
